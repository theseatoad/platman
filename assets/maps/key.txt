- Platman window is 960 by 768. 
- Tiles are 25 x 25.
- The playable area is 35 tiles x 25 tiles 


ID KEY:
0 = empty

1 = Double Line Top Left Corner
2 = Double Line Top Right Corner
3 = Double Line Bottom Right Corner
4 = Double Line Bottom Left Corner

5 = Double Line Flat wall Left / Right
6 = Double Line Flat wall Top / Bottom

11 = Single Line Top Left Corner
12 = Single Line Top Right Corner
13 = Single Line Bottom Right Corner
14 = Single Line Bottom Left Corner

15 = Double Line Flat wall Left / Right
16 = Double Line Flat wall Top / Bottom

17 = Single Line Full Left
18 = Single Line Full Right
